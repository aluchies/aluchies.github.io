Adam Luchies

Adam is a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He is a member of the Bioacoustics Research Laboratory, in which he studies acoustic and elastic wave propagation and ultrasound imaging with Michael Oelze. Adam is also an active member of the InterVarsity Graduate Christian Fellowship at Illinois.

Adam attended John Brown University for his bachelors degree in engineering. As a student, he worked as an engineering/math/physics tutor in student support services, was an active member of the honors program and the math team, and won an award for the best technical design for his senior design project.

Adam grew up in Overland Park, KS with his awesome family.
