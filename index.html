Adam Luchies

Adam is a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His research interests include quantitative ultrasound imaging, time series analysis, wave propagation in random media, and random field theory. He has taken courses in acoustic and electromagnetic wave propagation, signal and image processing, random processes, and detection and estimation theories. Adam attended John Brown University for his bachelors degree in engineering. As a student, he worked as an engineering/math/physics tutor in student support services, was an active member of the honors program and the math team, and won an award for the best technical design for his senior design project. Adam grew up in Overland Park, KS with his awesome family.
