Adam Luchies

Adam is a postdocoral fellow in Biomedical Engineering at Vanderbilt University. He is a member of the BEAM Lab, in which he studies ultrasound imaging with Brett Byram.

Adam completed his PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Michael Oelze.

Adam attended John Brown University for his bachelors degree in engineering. As a student, he worked as an engineering/math/physics tutor in student support services, was an active member of the honors program and the math team, and won an award for the best technical design for his senior design project.

Adam grew up in Overland Park, KS with his awesome family.
